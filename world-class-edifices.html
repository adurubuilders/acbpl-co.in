World Class Edifices


Superior construction standards

At ACBL our buildings fend for themselves, with a unique design, quality construction and a dedicated team that strives to deliver each project on time. With a history of large scale projects that have been accomplished and prestigious projects in tow, ACBL thrives on the goodwill earned by its clients. Over and above carrying on regular commercial and residential projects, our team is adept at identifying potential and transforming them into tangible projects that sell. At ACBL there is no dearth of overall infrastructure and resources that are required for execution of these projects and additionally take on new challenges.

We tread on a step by step meticulous route to success

  • The necessary blue print, calculations and preliminary design of the project is chalked out.
  • Engineering support to field operations is kicked off post review of the design and obtaining active feedback.
  • All stages of construction is actively supervised and carried out using world class construction material under strict supervision and tests at all stages.
  • Experienced and senior engineers are deployed to handle project management, to handle any surprises that spring up in the site and for overall support until complete execution.

Our core management team has a wealth of experience that ensures the right attention is paid to all the details of an impending project from the start to finish. This strife to perfection reflects in our output as a result. All our projects have been welcomed by our customers, who graciously pass on the good word thereby helping us grow.

The pursuit of excellence is a journey just begun for us at ACBL; we have miles to go indeed.
