ABOUT

Discover, Connect & Collaborate at TECHINNOVATION 2017
19 - 20 September 2017, Singapore

TECHINNOVATION is a premier technology-industry brokerage event organised by IPI. It brings together international and Singapore technology providers and seekers to explore technology and business collaboration opportunities through open innovation. In its sixth year since its inception in 2012, it has grown to become a leading conference and exhibition in Singapore that focuses on the matching of industry’s needs to enabling technologies.

Crowdsourcing

Seek collaborations and discover technologies through an open innovation platform.

Crowdpitching

Exhibit and pitch technologies for commercialisation or co-development to technology seekers.

Best Practices

Learn from thought leaders and be inspired by successful enterprises in their innovation journeys.

1-to-1 Meetings

Connect with potential collaborators through pre-matched business meetings.

Expand Your Innovation and Business Reach Through the Enterprise Europe Network (EEN), Sep 21, 1400 hrs

The EEN is the world's largest business and innovation support platform to promote business partnership, technology transfer and research collaboration between SMEs in the European Union and the rest of the world. Food Innovation Presentations

In recent years, socioeconomic trends and technology advancements have reshaped how food is produced, prepared and distributed to consumers. While traditional value drivers of taste and price remain important, consumers’ preferences have shifted towards products that are clean-label, convenient, nutritious, sustainable and ensured of food quality and safety. In the face of such consumer-led demands combined with the challenges of manpower, productivity, rising costs and strong competition in the global market, it is essential that food companies leverage technology to remain relevant and stay ahead of the curve. Join us for insight on some of the latest innovations taking place in the food industry. Learn how companies are using technology to create innovative products and solutions to meet the industry’s evolving needs.
