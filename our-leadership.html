Our Leadership


A leader constantly raises the bar of performance

It is undeniably a great leap from the domain of health care to a tougher real estate and property development; for such is the grit and determination exhibited by our dynamic duo of the founder and his better half. This attitude has indeed trickled down through all levels of the management and various units which form the enterprise; taking risks. Out of the box thinking and independent decision making is encouraged here at ACBL. Progressive thinking is that which helps an organization grow; and more so If it begins at a grass root level.

A leader leads by example, motivates the team to perform better and ensures that delivery of a project first conform to expected standards. The team at ACBL is first led to

  • Arrive at standards of the project based on requirements of the client with respect to the property- we attempt at exceeding them.
  • Complete commitment at each stage of project completion by the exclusive tem thus assigned.
  • Implementation of crystal clear processes and use of branded construction material-we do not scrimp on costs here.
  • Elimination of any construction related delays.

When there are stringent supervisory measures in place, is helps the team at ACBL carry out projects with least room for delays in delivery. Perfection in ACBL is an acquired habit, our leadership ensures that.
