Infrastructure

Supporting Growth

ACBPL constructions was established in 2012, by a dynamic promoter with a vision to excel, and has through consistent growth and quality performance, become a leading name in the construction industry. Having expertise in developing ultra modern industrial constructions, along with high tech commercial complexes and premium residential apartments, ACBPL is a competent and qualified source to meet the client’s complete infrastructure needs. Projects developed by us are mainly turnkey facilities made as per the specifications of the client and offered to them in build, operate and transfer basis. We manage every step as per design and while monitoring cost, constructability and schedule. Once construction is commenced we manage progress, quality and final delivery.

ACBPL takes pride in being a multidisciplinary consultation organization chiefly in the fields of infrastructure and related technologies. Our scope and ambit encompasses, design, planning and construction of major infrastructure projects like Airports, Bridge and Tunnel Engineering, Construction management, Conceptualization and design of Rail Based transportations systems, Construction, operation and maintenance of Highways, expressways, feeders roads, bridges, flyovers, road network and upgrading of road systems.

Portfolio of projects ACBPL is involved comprise

  • Commercial Ventures

  • Residential Undertakings / Villas / Gated Communities

  • Rail Infrastructure

  • Hospitals

  • Hotels and Resorts

  • Industrial Buildings

  • Government and Public Buildings

  • Diagnostic Centres

  • Project Management
