Services


Our services encompass a complete range of services right from land identification, laying down design and turnkey execution of the same. ACBL is committed to providing a broad spectrum of integrated solutions in real estate and construction of both residential and commercial buildings.  By means of implementing state of the art and latest technologies in all the projects undertaken; our buildings stand out for great care and detailing. While striving to meet and exceed customer expectations, ACBL also is sensitive to the environment and works in the eco-friendly direction. ACBL is equipped to handle large scale commercial as well as residential projects, and successfully execute them in line with client approved strategies. On other hand, ACBL is flexible enough to also take on promotion and development of properties belonging to a third party. Additional commercial services are also provided as an added advantage!

The team has ably carried out all kinds of projects covering prestigious construction projects from luxury high rise residential buildings, large corporate constructions serving all kinds of sectors. The same dedication and efforts are deployed on small scale projects as well, at ACBL “work is truly worship” each project matters. Meticulously thought out blue prints, stringent quality measures, the efforts of a completely dedicated team with minute attention to detail reflect in all our endeavors.

In a nut shell we cover the following sectors

  • Residential
  • Commercial and industrial estate development
  • Hospitality & Leisure
  • Diagnostic centers
  • Project Management
  • Over and above all this turnkey services

We also actively engage in philanthropic activities and are associated with M/s Sakthi Sowjanya trust so as to work with the under-privileged.

Our Modus-operandi

ACBL assigns owners to an exclusive architect and a contractor for each project; thus the client completely has an expert team at his disposal until completion. There is thus complete focus and uniform attention paid to each client. The fast track construction methods that are deployed brings down the time required for the completion of a given construction project while permitting simultaneous execution of related tasks under the same project. Thus we deliver our projects on time, you may rest assured on that.

At ACBL all our building strategies are worked out bearing in mind customer expectations, his dreams and aspirations on the property. There certainly no short route to success, ACBL has been able to grow with each project, ready to take on challenges of a new dimension each time.  The efforts indeed have paid off in terms of a diversified and solid portfolio to boast about!
