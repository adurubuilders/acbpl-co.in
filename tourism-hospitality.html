Tourism and Hospitality Infrastructure

Playing a critical Role in Tourism

ACBL has comprehensive knowledge of the Tourism assets and strengths of the Indian states and the imperative need for accommodation in different classes of hotels to suit the varying requirements of tourists at different destinations. . Accommodation and facilities play a very important part in the popularity of any tourist destination. Hence the tourist and the hotel industry now function in tandem as the Tourism and Hospitality industry. The best possible room and restaurant services as well as well themed resorts are in great demand for the allure of the tourists. ACBL now ventures to cater to this demand and take up major Tourism projects to manage the increasing tourist influx.

Presently, a great shortage of accommodation is still being experienced in the tourist circuits all over the country because of a constant growth in the traffic of all categories of tourists. ACBL intends to partner the tourist industry on Public Private Partnership basis, with the state Governments by developing Resorts, motels, tourist bungalows, service apartments and lodges for seasonal, shorter or longer stays. In addition to the usual provision of high level facilities and comforts, ACBL is capable of developing hotels and luxury resorts of international standards with large conference halls, communication linkage with internet facilities, recreational environment and even health clubs. ACBL aims to leave no stone unturned in giving prospective tourists the best comforts possible.
