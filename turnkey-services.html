Turnkey Services


In the world of superior civil engineering and construction, turnkey services simply mean that the client is simply required to “turn” “key” for occupation of the property.

ACBL undertakes construction of the actual outer structure, works on tenant space (including finishes on flooring and counters), installation of appliances and furniture specific to include any equipment to the tenant's use. Thus the customer is simply left to occupy the establishment and commence business. The building is complete as per plan and furnished as per the requirement of the customer.

At ACBL, we completely understand the pulse of the client and take on projects with complete confidence of completing the same to customer satisfaction.

Turnkey services are our forte`-

  • A contractual network is put in place; ACBL outsources any scope of additional work in the project to other subcontractors and suppliers. As indicated earlier a complete team is assigned to each project that oversees work in progress at each stage thereby delivering projects on time.
  • We provide clear value analysis of site plans, recommending optimal project specifications, reporting potential project pitfalls, project schedule management, and periodic inspection of sight work and ensure complete management and co-ordination within the site.
  • ACBL exudes confidence and expertise in each aspect of such projects leaving future owners of the property completely satisfied. You may inspect some of our samples that are ready.

Our lives are increasingly fast-paced, products and services are packaged to suit convenience and such comforts are being welcomed. Your “complete package” of a property lies here with us at ACBL.
